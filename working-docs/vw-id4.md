# Volkswagen ID.4 — Assessment

_Last updated: March 2026_
_Verdict: **Solid supplementary option — same shortlist, more listings to browse**_

## Why it's here

The ID.4 is not materially better than the Enyaq (they share the same MEB platform and similar pricing), but adding it to the search pool means more listings on Marktplaats and AutoScout24. It's a well-known, well-supported car with a large used market in the Netherlands, so good deals appear regularly.

One notable advantage over the Enyaq: **higher trims have an electric tailgate**, which the Enyaq never offers.

## How it compares to the Enyaq

Both are built on VW Group's MEB platform, so they're mechanically very similar. Key differences:

| | ID.4 Style | Enyaq Edition |
|---|---|---|
| Electric tailgate | ✅ | ❌ |
| Boot | 543 L | 585 L |
| Tri-zone climate | ❌ | ✅ |
| Rear window blinds | ❌ | ✅ |
| Pre-heating w/o charging | ❌ | ✅ |
| Used price | €21,000–25,000 | €20,000–24,000 |
| VW dealer network | ✅ Excellent | ✅ Good |

**If the electric tailgate matters:** ID.4 Style wins over Enyaq Edition.
**If boot space and tri-zone climate matter more:** Enyaq L&K wins.

## Charging

Same 400V, 135 kW DC as the Enyaq — 10–80% in approximately 35 minutes. Not as fast as the 800V Korean cars but perfectly acceptable for the routes involved (UK/Belgium).

## Reliability and ownership

VW Group's MEB platform is well-understood by now, with a large dealer network across the Netherlands. Software updates have resolved many early issues. Generally considered reliable.

- Heat pump: **not standard on all trims** — always verify on a specific listing
- 12V auxiliary battery: similar mild concern to Ioniq 5/EV6; ask about service history

## What to filter on Marktplaats

- Model: ID.4
- Fuel: Elektrisch
- Year: 2021+
- Trim: Style or Max (or filter by price €21,000–27,000 and check trim in listing)
- Battery: confirm 77 kWh (not the older 52 kWh entry models)
