# Volvo EX60 — Assessment

_Last updated: March 2026_
_Verdict: **Not viable — doesn't exist yet**_

## The situation

The EX60 was revealed on January 21, 2026 — just two months ago. It is not available to buy. Orders open in late spring 2026, test drives in the second half of 2026, and production starts in Gothenburg in the first half of 2026. Used examples won't exist for years.

## Why it's interesting (for the future)

On paper, the EX60 looks very strong:

- 800V platform → 10–80% in ~18 minutes
- Three powertrain options: 83 kWh (369 hp), 95 kWh (503 hp), 117 kWh (670 hp)
- WLTP range up to ~640 km on larger battery
- Volvo's reputation for safety and Scandinavian interior quality
- Built in Gothenburg (European production, should help supply)

If you were buying new in 18–24 months, it would be worth a serious look.

## Why it doesn't work now

You can't buy one. And even when it does become available, new Volvos at this spec level typically start at €50,000–65,000+. Used examples at affordable prices are years away.

## Verdict

Ruled out — not available. Keep an eye on it if you revisit the decision in 2–3 years, but it's irrelevant for a purchase in 2026.
