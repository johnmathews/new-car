## Home charging

### Why it matters

For 20,000 km/year of mostly short local trips, you'd charge at home almost every night. At ‚Ç¨0.22/kWh (assumed), your daily top-up costs roughly ‚Ç¨1‚Äì2. This is the core of the financial saving vs petrol.

### Wallbox installation

A home wallbox is strongly recommended over a standard 3-pin socket:
- Standard socket (2.3 kW): charges ~10 km/hour ‚Äî too slow for regular use
- Wallbox (11 kW, three-phase): charges ~60‚Äì65 km/hour ‚Äî full charge overnight

**Estimated wallbox cost in NL:**
- Wallbox unit: ‚Ç¨400‚Äì800 (e.g. Easee, Alfen Eve, Zaptec)
- Installation by certified electrician: ‚Ç¨300‚Äì600 (depends on cable run, existing wiring)
- **Total: ‚Ç¨700‚Äì1,400**

Some energy suppliers offer subsidised or included wallbox installation. Worth checking with your current supplier.

### Spaarndam-specific question

This is an open item. Key questions:
- Is the property owned or rented? (Landlord permission needed if renting)
- Is there space to mount a wallbox near where the car parks?
- Is the main electrical panel (meterkast) modern enough to support 3-phase charging? (Most NL homes built after ~1990 are fine)
- Is there a dedicated parking spot, or shared/street parking?

_Annotation: Can you park adjacent to the house/garage? Do you have a private parking spot?_

### Smart charging

- Charge during off-peak hours (e.g. 11pm‚Äì6am) at a lower tariff

## Holiday trips ‚Äî UK and Belgium

### Belgium

Excellent charging infrastructure, especially along motorways. Fast chargers every 30‚Äì50 km on major routes. No issues for Ioniq 5 / EV6 (800V platform = fast stops). Plan a 15‚Äì20 min stop every 200‚Äì250 km.

### UK

Improving rapidly but still less dense than NL. Key networks: BP Pulse, Osprey, Pod Point, Gridserve. The Ioniq 5 and EV6 charge at up to 233 kW on compatible UK chargers (CCS standard, same as Europe). Recommended to plan stops using Chargemap or ABRP (A Better Route Planner) before driving.

Note: The UK has moved to CCS as standard ‚Äî no adapter needed.

### Route planning apps

- **ABRP (A Better Route Planner)** ‚Äî best for EV-specific routing, accounts for real-world range, elevation, weather
- **Chargemap** ‚Äî good for finding individual chargers, user reviews
- **PlugShare** ‚Äî community-driven, good for finding working chargers
- Car's built-in nav (Ioniq 5 / EV6) ‚Äî includes charging stop planning and can pre-condition battery before arrival at fast charger

## V2L ‚Äî Vehicle to Load (Ioniq 5 Ultimate / EV6 GT-Line S)
