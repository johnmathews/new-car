# Trim Level Guide

_Last updated: April 2026_

A quick reference for which trim level to look for when browsing used listings. All prices are approximate used market prices in the Netherlands (March 2026).

---

## Hyundai Ioniq 5

| Trim | Used price | Key features | Verdict |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| SE Connect | €20,000–23,000 | Predictive ACC (NSCC), blind spot, traffic sign recognition, electric tailgate, 8" display | Too basic — no pano roof or 360° cam |
| Connect | €22,000–25,000 | Adds 12" nav display | Still no pano roof |
| **Lounge** ★ | **€24,000–28,000** | **Pano roof, 360° camera, heat pump, larger wheels** | **Sweet spot — gets all the key wishlist items** |
| Ultimate | €27,000–32,000 | Leather seats, HUD, Smart Parking Assist (SPA), V2L | Nice if budget allows; leather and SPA are the main adds |

**What to look for on listings:** Lounge or higher. SE Connect and Connect miss the panoramic roof and 360° camera. Note: predictive ACC (NSCC), blind spot detection, and traffic sign recognition are standard on ALL trims — no need to check for these.

**Note:** Heat pump is standard from Lounge trim. Always verify on the specific listing.

**Minimum acceptable:** Lounge

## Kia EV6

| Trim | Used price | Key features | Verdict |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Air | €20,000–23,000 | Predictive ACC (NSCC-C), blind spot, traffic sign recognition, electric tailgate | Too basic — no pano, no camera |
| Wind | €23,000–26,000 | Adds 360° camera, larger battery standard | Closer but still missing pano roof |
| **GT-Line** ★ | **€27,000–32,000** | **Pano roof, Smart Parking Assist (SPA), Meridian audio, leather standard** | **Sweet spot — but stretches budget** |
| GT-Line S | €32,000–38,000 | Adds cooled seats, HUD, 21" wheels | Over budget |

**What to look for on listings:** GT-Line. Wind is acceptable but misses the panoramic roof and SPA. GT-Line includes leather as standard (not an option), which is a bonus. Note: predictive ACC (NSCC-C), blind spot detection, and traffic sign recognition are standard on ALL trims.

**Note:** The EV6 GT-Line consistently costs €3,000–5,000 more than an equivalent Ioniq 5 Lounge. Worth keeping in mind.

**Minimum acceptable:** Wind (if GT-Line is over budget, noting no pano roof and no SPA)

## Skoda Enyaq iV

| Trim | Used price | Key features | Verdict |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Enyaq iV (base) | €18,000–20,000 | Basic spec, limited options | Too basic |
| **Edition** ★ | **€20,000–24,000** | **Rear blinds, better standard spec** | **Entry point for sensible spec** |
| Sportline | €22,000–26,000 | Sporty styling, matrix LED headlights | If you prefer the sporty look |
| **L&K** ★ | **€25,000–30,000** | **Leather seats, massage, Canton audio** | **Best spec — still no electric tailgate** |

**What to look for on listings:** Edition minimum; L&K if budget allows for the leather and audio. **Critical: check which option packages are fitted** — on pre-2025 Enyaq, predictive ACC (pACC) requires the Assisted Drive package, blind spot requires Side Assist, and Park Assist requires Parking Plus. These are three separate packages. Business Edition often has all three. See enyaq.md for full details.

**Important reminder:** No pre-facelift Enyaq trim has an electric tailgate. Traffic sign recognition is standard on all trims. If the listing has ACC, it has predictive ACC (pACC) — the hardware is predictive by design.

**Minimum acceptable:** Edition (with Assisted Drive + Side Assist packages at minimum)

## Tips when browsing listings

1. **Always check the trim level explicitly** — sellers sometimes don't list it correctly, or list a car as "fully loaded" when it's a mid-spec.
2. **Cross-reference with the VIN or option codes** — Hyundai and Kia dealers can look up the original spec from the VIN. Useful for second-hand verification.
3. **Look for factory sticker / window sticker** — some sellers have the original order sheet which lists every option.
4. **Marktplaats filter tips:** Filter by year (2021+), fuel type (elektrisch), and set a max price. Then manually check trim level from the listing description.
5. **AutoScout24** is also worth checking alongside Marktplaats — sometimes different inventory.
